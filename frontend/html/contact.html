Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to urgent pest issues?

We offer same-day emergency service for urgent pest issues in most Adelaide suburbs. For non-urgent inquiries, we typically respond within 1-2 business hours.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all pest control services. We can often provide an estimate over the phone, but for more complex issues, we may schedule a brief inspection to ensure accurate pricing.

What areas in Adelaide do you service?

We service all metropolitan Adelaide suburbs, from Gawler in the north to Seaford in the south, and from the hills to the beaches. For locations outside these areas, please contact us to check availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, direct bank transfers, and cash payments. Payment is typically required upon completion of service, and we provide electronic invoices and receipts.
