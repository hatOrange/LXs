About Us

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

LX Pest Solutions was founded in 2010 by Alex Brown, a certified pest management professional with a passion for providing effective solutions that prioritize environmental responsibility.

What began as a small local operation has grown into one of Adelaide’s most trusted pest control services, with a team of certified professionals serving both residential and commercial clients citywide.

Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to exceptional service, environmental stewardship, and guaranteed results—principles guiding every aspect of our operations.

15+ Years of Experience
12,500+ Satisfied Customers
98% Success Rate

Our Values & Certifications

  • Eco-Friendly & Sustainable Treatments: We use environmentally responsible products and methods that effectively eliminate pests while minimizing ecological impact.
  • Certified Professionals & Guaranteed Service: Our technicians undergo ongoing training to remain at the forefront of pest-control technology.
  • Rapid Response & Modern Technology: We leverage advanced pest-monitoring systems and digital reporting to provide faster, more effective service.
  • Transparency & Education: We empower customers by educating them about pest prevention and management best practices.
  • Community Involvement: We actively participate in local environmental initiatives and offer educational workshops to schools and community groups.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, environmentally responsible pest management services that protect homes, businesses, and public health—backed by guaranteed results and excellent customer care.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Adelaide’s premier sustainable pest management provider, setting the standard for effectiveness, environmental stewardship, and client satisfaction.
